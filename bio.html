Bubbles

​ ​

Bubbles is one of the two co-protagonists (alongside Buttercup) of the animated television series The Powerpuff Girls. She fights crime with her sisters Blossom and Buttercup.

She is the Joy and the Laughter; she is the emotional glue of the superhero trio. She is the "sugar" ingredient of the three.
